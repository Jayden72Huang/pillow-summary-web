## Description

**Pillow Summary** turns your "read later" graveyard into actual knowledge.

Here's how it works:
1. Set a learning goal ("Learn React in 30 days")
2. Save articles, videos, tweets throughout the day
3. Every evening, get a 3-minute AI knowledge brief — only the insights that matter for YOUR goal

**Why we built this:**
After Pocket shut down (17M users stranded) and Omnivore closed, we realized the problem isn't saving — it's actually reading. Every "read later" app makes saving easy but reading hard. We flipped it: AI does the reading, you get the knowledge.

**Key features:**
- Goal-driven reading — every article scored by relevance to your goal
- AI daily brief — 3-minute personalized knowledge digest
- Learning streaks — gentle habit tracking
- Bedtime mode — warm, calm design for evening reading
- Your data, your control — full export, always

**Try the live demo** — paste any URL and see AI extract what matters. No signup needed.
